Paris, France


Paris, the capital of France, is known for its stunning architecture, world-class museums, and rich history. Often referred to as "The City of Light," Paris offers a unique blend of culture, romance, and modernity, making it one of the most popular holiday destinations in the world.

Top 5 Attractions in Paris

  1. Eiffel Tower - An iconic symbol of Paris and a must-see landmark.
  2. Louvre Museum - Home to thousands of works of art, including the Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral - A stunning example of Gothic architecture.
  4. Montmartre - A charming district known for its bohemian atmosphere and the Sacré-Cœur Basilica.
  5. Champs-Élysées - One of the most famous avenues in the world, lined with theaters, cafés, and shops.

Must-try Foods in Paris

  • Croissant - A buttery, flaky pastry often enjoyed with coffee.
  • Escargot - Snails cooked in garlic butter, a French delicacy.
  • Baguette - A traditional French loaf of bread, perfect for sandwiches.
  • Macarons - Colorful, delicate cookies filled with cream or ganache.
  • Crêpes - Thin pancakes filled with a variety of sweet or savory fillings.
